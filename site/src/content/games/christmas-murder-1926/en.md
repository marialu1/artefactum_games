## The Setting

Manhattan, 1926. The jazz is loud, the champagne is flowing, and the secrets are deadly.

Eleven guests have gathered at the legendary Gatsby Rose Manor for a Christmas Eve soirée. The host, the mysterious **Rose Gatsby**, has promised an evening none will forget.

She kept her promise. Before midnight, one guest lies dead.

## What You Get

- **11 Character Dossiers** — Complete backgrounds, secrets, and motivations
- **26 Clues** — Physical evidence across 3 tiers of discovery
- **Game Master Script** — Full evening timeline and revelation guide
- **Printable Materials** — Invitations, menu cards, name tags
- **Soundtrack Playlist** — Curated 1920s jazz and ambient music
- **Host Guide** — Setup instructions, food pairing suggestions, decoration tips

## How It Works

1. **Invite your guests** — Each receives a character assignment 1-2 weeks before
2. **Set the scene** — Transform your space into 1926 Manhattan
3. **Play the evening** — Dinner, drinks, and deadly deductions
4. **Unmask the killer** — The final accusation
