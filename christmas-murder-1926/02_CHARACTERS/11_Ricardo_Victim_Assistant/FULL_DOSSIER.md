## REGGIE - CHARACTER ESSENCE

### **Who Is Reginald Ashford III?**

**In Three Sentences:**

Reginald "Reggie" Ashford III is a **48-year-old textile magnate** who built his fortune through ruthless business practices, strategic marriages, and complete disregard for anyone standing in his way - a man who abandoned his own twin daughters at birth, destroyed careers for sport, and treats his wife Helena like a possession rather than a partner. He's **charming in public** (quick wit, generous host, magnetic personality) but **cruel in private** (emotional abuse, calculated cruelties, zero empathy), creating a perfect storm where absolutely everyone at his Christmas Eve party has a **legitimate reason to want him dead**. His murder at **20h11** is both tragic (a human life ended) and inevitable (he made too many enemies) - and as the victim, his job tonight is to **make that murder feel JUSTIFIED** to the audience.

### **Core Paradox**

**"The Charming Monster"**

Reggie is NOT a cartoon villain. He's worse: **believably awful**. 

He tells jokes that make you laugh before you realize they were insults. He gives compliments that feel like slaps. He's generous (throws expensive parties) but cruel (uses them to humiliate guests). He loves his wife (obsessively possessive) but destroys her (emotional torture).

The paradox: You WANT to like him (charisma!) but you CAN'T (cruelty!). And that makes his murder feel... deserved.

### **Player Experience Goal**

*"I must make everyone want me dead in 10 minutes. Be insufferable but not cartoonish. Plant motives. Then die gracefully and become a completely different character for the rest of the night. No pressure!"*
