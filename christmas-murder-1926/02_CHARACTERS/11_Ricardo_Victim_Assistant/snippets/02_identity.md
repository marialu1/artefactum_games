**From Reggie to Clarence:**

**Physical Changes:**
- Costume (suit → butler uniform)
- Posture (big → small)
- Expression (arrogant → neutral)

**Character Changes:**
- Owner → Servant
- Loud → Quiet
- Cruel → Kind
- Center → Background

**Key Difference:**

**Reggie:** Takes up space, commands attention

**Clarence:** Disappears, serves invisibly
