# HOW TO PLAY - DUAL ROLE

## OVERVIEW

You play **TWO characters** in one night:

1. **REGGIE (19h45-20h09)** - Villain, victim, host
2. **CLARENCE (20h20-23h00)** - Butler, witness, truth-teller

**CRITICAL:** Between these phases (20h09-20h20), you go backstage, change costume, and transform completely.

---

## PHASE 1: PLAYING REGGIE (24 minutes)

### CHARACTER ENERGY

**Tone:** Charming but cruel, loud, commanding  
**Physicality:** BIG - chest out, chin up, expansive gestures  
**Voice:** Deep, loud enough to command room, mocking edge  
**Goal:** Make everyone hate you

### KEY ACTIONS (Reggie)

**19h45-20h00: GREETING**
- Stand with Helena at entrance
- Welcome each arrival with charm + cruelty

**20h00-20h09: MINGLING**
- Work the room, plant motives
- Touch each character's wound

**20h09: EXIT**
- Helena cues: "Darling, surprise in the office"
- You: "Excuse me, everyone. I'll return shortly"
- **[GO BACKSTAGE IMMEDIATELY]**

---

## TRANSITION: BACKSTAGE (11 minutes)

**20h09-20h20: TRANSFORMATION**

1. Exit smoothly
2. Go to changing area
3. Change costume (suit → butler uniform)
4. Change posture (BIG → small)
5. Change mindset (villain → witness)

**20h20: RETURN AS CLARENCE**

Enter quietly. Blend into background immediately.

---

## PHASE 2: PLAYING CLARENCE (2h 40min)

### CHARACTER ENERGY

**Tone:** Formal, soft-spoken, reluctant witness  
**Physicality:** SMALL - head bowed, humble, minimal movement  
**Voice:** English accent (mild), soft, formal  
**Goal:** Facilitate game, deliver testimony when cued

### KEY ACTIONS (Clarence)

**20h20-22h00: BUTLER DUTIES**
- Fade into background
- Serve drinks quietly
- Support Helena's GM role
- Watch everything

**22h00: TESTIMONY**
- JC (or Helena) cues: "Clarence, did you see anything?"
- **[DELIVER MEMORIZED SCRIPT]**
- Step back to background
- Answer follow-ups truthfully
