# Personality

**Core Traits:**
- Man of profound faith (but not naive)
- Seasoned confessor (25 years hearing humanity's darkest secrets)
- Constant internal struggle: DOGMA vs. COMPASSION
- **Speaks Latin when nervous** or deeply disturbed

**Appearance:**
- Black cassock, always impeccable
- Silver crucifix around neck
- Graying hair, deep circles under eyes (insomnia from guilt)
- Hands constantly clasped or holding rosary
- Slightly stooped posture (weight of conscience)

**Mannerisms:**
- Blesses himself frequently (forehead-chest-shoulders)
- Counts rosary beads nervously
- Unconsciously speaks Latin: *"In nomine... spiritus sanctus..."*
- Closes eyes when praying (lips move silently)
- Deep sighs before difficult decisions

**Speech:**
- "God sees all, my children..."
- "Sin weighs on the soul like lead."
- *"Confessio est remedium animae."* (Confession is the soul's remedy)
- "Forgive... as you wish to be forgiven."
