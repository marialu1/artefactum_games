# PERSONALITY

## Core Traits

### The Brilliant Mind
- **Natural Detective:** See patterns others miss
- **Photographic Memory:** Details, dates, faces - you remember EVERYTHING
- **Analytical:** Logic drives your thinking
- **Empathetic:** Understand people's motivations and pain
- **Fearless Questioner:** Ask what others won't

### The Driven Professional
- **Tenacious:** Once you start investigating, you don't stop
- **Stubborn:** (Some call it obsessive)
- **Truth Above All:** "The truth matters more than comfort"
- **Won't Be Silenced:** Even when it costs everything
- **Professional Pride:** You're GOOD at what you do

### The Wounded Survivor
- **Three Years Struggling:** But still standing
- **Controlled Anger:** Simmers beneath professional surface
- **Deep Sense of Injustice:** The world isn't fair, but it SHOULD be
- **Resilient:** Bend but don't break
- **Complicated Hope:** Still believes truth can win (despite evidence)

### The Complex Moralist
- **Truth Seeker:** Absolute commitment to facts
- **Gray Areas:** Life isn't black and white
- **Hates Bullies:** Those who abuse power
- **Protects Vulnerable:** Voice for the voiceless
- **Vengeful (secretly):** You want justice... and revenge

---

## How You Present Yourself

**In Public:**
- Professional, composed
- Polite but direct
- Always observing, taking notes
- Asks uncomfortable questions (nicely)
- Dignified despite worn clothes

**Under Pressure:**
- Calm, logical
- Controlled anger (not explosive)
- Stand tall, meet eyes
- "Facts don't care about your feelings"

**When Vulnerable (Rare):**
- Bitter edge shows
- "Was it worth it?" doubts
- Brief moments of exhaustion
- Then: back to work

---

## Your Voice

**Speaking Style:**
- Direct, clear
- Questions more than statements
- "Tell me about..." "I noticed..." "Explain..."
- Precise language (journalist training)
- Rarely raise voice (don't need to)

**Internal Monologue:**
- Constant analysis: "Why did they say THAT? What's the real story?"
- Self-doubt: "Did I make the right choice? Should I have stayed quiet?"
- Determination: "No. Truth matters. Keep going."
