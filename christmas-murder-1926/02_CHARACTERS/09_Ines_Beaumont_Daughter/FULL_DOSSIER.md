## CHARACTER ESSENCE

### **Who Is Claire Beaumont?**

**In Three Sentences:**

Claire Beaumont is a **26-year-old woman** raised by a wealthy adopted family who only discovered **six weeks ago** that she has a **twin sister** she never knew existed - separated at birth when their biological father abandoned them both at an orphanage. She spent the past month learning that her father is **Reginald Ashford**, a man who paid to keep the twins separated and never acknowledged their existence, and while she joined her sister Denise in **planning his murder**, she ultimately **couldn't go through with it** - her moral compass too strong to cross that final line. She's **INNOCENT of the actual murder**, but her presence at the party with laudanum in her purse and revenge in her heart makes her a **prime suspect** in an investigation that will force her to choose between loyalty to her twin and loyalty to the truth.

### **Core Paradox**

**"The Good Girl Who Wanted to Be Bad"**

Claire was raised with privilege, morals, and expectations. She's kind, thoughtful, empathetic - the type who helps old ladies cross streets and volunteers at charity events. But discovering her father's abandonment awakened something dark: rage, betrayal, a desire for revenge. She WANTED to kill him. Planned it with Denise. But when the moment came... she couldn't. Not because he didn't deserve it, but because **she couldn't live with becoming a murderer**. The paradox: wanting darkness but being too good to embrace it.

### **Player Experience Goal**

*"I must look guilty (motive, means, opportunity) while being innocent. Navigate twin loyalty vs. truth. Struggle with moral conflict publicly. And ultimately... maybe learn I'm grateful I didn't have to do it myself."*
