## THE SURVIVOR WHO KEPT SURVIVING

Esmeralda Beaumont fought for 26 years.

Fought for food. Fought for safety. Fought for respect.

Fought to survive when the world wanted her to disappear.

Then found her twin.

First person who loved her.

First person worth protecting.

And discovered their father: the man who stole 26 years from them.

She wanted revenge. Planned murder. Ready to execute.

But someone else did it first.

**Cheated of her justice.**

Or... **saved from becoming what she feared?**

She doesn't know yet.

What she knows:

She survived the orphanage.

She survived poverty.

She survived abandonment.

**She'll survive this too.**

Because that's what Denise does.
